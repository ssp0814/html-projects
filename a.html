    FAQs

    How do I find nearby charging stations? ⌄

    Use the Station Locator above. Tap “Near Me” to detect your location and view the closest chargers with directions.

    What charger types are supported? ⌄

    We support AC Type 2 (7.4–22 kW) and DC CCS2 (30–150 kW). Most modern Indian EVs are compatible.

    How are charging rates calculated? ⌄

    Pricing is per-kWh and varies by station and time of day. The app shows an estimate before you start.

    What amenities are available? ⌄

    Amenities vary by site and can include parking, restrooms, café/food, and 24×7 access.
